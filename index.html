York vs Lancaster
Roses Revival App Jam
2nd May 11am-5pm

The jam has now taken place!

Participants were tasked with building something based around the Reddit API. We had some really creative submissions!

Thank you to everybody who took part. Stay tuned for any more events in the future!

Are you a keen or budding programmer?

Lancaster and York have come together this May 2nd to host the Roses Revival App Jam! Either individually or in teams, you will have 6 hours to design and create an app based off an existing API.

Submissions will be marked according to the following criteria:

  • Efficiency (10%)
  • Code Structure (10%)
  • Design/Approach to the task (50%)
  • Use of the API (30%)

Each section will be marked out of 10 and scaled by the weighting of that section.

We will be opening a Slack server that you will be free to join if you wish to talk to others or receive support for the App Jam. Though, feel free to create your own groups/servers on whichever platforms you prefer.

The chosen API and judges are yet to be announced and this page will be updated as such.

Good luck, and happy coding!
